ABOUT ME

I discovered my passion for pastry at the age of 17 while on an exchange program in France. That experience inspired me to pursue formal training at Gastronomicom, a prestigious pastry school in the south of France. I had the privilege of interning at the Beau Rivage Hotel near Lyon, and during my time in Europe, I worked alongside some incredible pastry chefs, including Sadaharu Aoki and Hugues Pouget in Paris, as well as Laurent Gerbaud in Brussels.

In 2011, I returned to Thailand and took on the role of Executive Pastry Chef at Issaya Siamese Club, an award-winning restaurant. Following the success of Issaya Siamese Club, I co-founded Issaya Cooking Studio in 2014, a well-regarded cooking school and event space, and later opened Issaya La Pâtisserie, a café at EmQuartier, in 2015. In 2016, I collaborated with Chef Ian Kittichai to co-author Issaya La Pâtisserie Pastry Cookbook.

Throughout my career, I’ve dedicated myself to continuous learning, completing workshops and internships with some of the world’s top pastry chefs, such as Jordi Bordas and Davide Malizia. It’s been a pleasure to welcome pastry stars like Amaury Guichon, Luc Debove, and Will Goldfarb to Issaya Cooking Studio as well.

In 2018, I was honored to be the first Thai national to compete in the Ladies World Pastry Championship in Rimini, Italy. That same year, I became the Pastry Chef of Saawaan, a fine-dining restaurant that has been awarded a MICHELIN STAR in 2019, 2020, and 2021. I was also thrilled to present the desserts for the MICHELIN Guide Bangkok Star Revelation and Gala Dinner.

In 2019, I co-founded ICI, a creative pastry café, and had the opportunity to present the dessert for the MICHELIN Guide Singapore Star Revelation and Gala Dinner.
